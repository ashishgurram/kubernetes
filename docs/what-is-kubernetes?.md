## Introduction
This document introduces Kubernetes, its official definition, the problems it solves, and the features of container orchestration tools.

## official defination
- Open source container orchestration tool
- developed by google
- Helps you manage containereised applications in different deployment environments
  
  - Physical
  - virtual
  - cloud

## What problems does k8s solve? & what are the tasks of an orchestration tool?

- The need for container orchestration tool
  - trend from monolith to microservices
  - increased usage of container (which can be very large and managing was difficult and thats why we need a proper way to mange those 100s of containers)
  
## What features do rochestration tools offer
- High availabilty or no downtimw
- scalability or high performance
- DR -  backup and restore
